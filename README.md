# Stephan Lukasczyk

> Researcher, nerd, music lover, dad

In professional life, I am a researcher in the 
[Software Testing Research team](https://lp.jetbrains.com/research/software-testing/) 
at [JetBrains Research](https://jetbrains.com/research).  My research interests range
from automated test generation to software quality and software testing.  Before
joining JetBrains Research, I was a research assistant and PhD student at the
[Chair of Software Engineering II](https://www.fim.uni-passau.de/lehrstuhl-fuer-software-engineering-ii)
at the [University of Passau](https://uni-passau.de), Germany, where I worked on
automated test generation for Python and (together with many others) brought the
[Pynguin](https://www.pynguin.eu) test-generation framework for Python to life.

I also voluntarily serve as the Treasurer of [DANTE e.V.](https://www.dante.de), the
German-speaking TeX users group.

I used to play the drums and piano, which I should definitely intensify again.  My
taste of music spans from classical music, to jazz, and metal.  Most likely, if you
would tell me about an arbitrary song that's popular on today's mainstream radio, I
have never heard it—and I am usually not regretting.
